# 🛡️ Oraya Database: Rules of Engagement

Hello Developer,

Oraya Database is more than just code; it's a commitment to performance, 
aesthetics, and open collaboration. To ensure this project remains a high-
quality resource for everyone, we have established these fundamental rules.

---

## Article 1: Branding & Identity (Our Core)
Our name is our honor.
- **Section 1.1**: The name "Oraya Database" must remain visible and unaltered 
  in all UI dashboards, footers, and metadata.
- **Section 1.2**: In any fork or hosted version, you must display: 
  "Powered by Oraya Database".
- **Section 1.3**: You may not claim authorship of the core Oraya engine.

## Article 2: Non-Monetization (Our Ethics)
Oraya is free, and it stays free.
- **Section 2.1**: Selling Oraya Database or charging access fees is strictly 
  prohibited.
- **Section 2.2**: You may build services *around* Oraya (consulting, hosting 
  setup), but the software itself must never be a paid product.

## Article 3: Open Continuity (Our Growth)
- **Section 3.1**: All modifications to the source code must be shared under 
  these same "Oraya" terms.
- **Section 3.2**: Private internal use is permitted, but any public-facing 
  service or distribution must be open source.

---

**Oraya Database — Data with Honor. Built by Developers, for Developers.**
